# AI Music Composition Prompts

## 7 Emotion Templates (Bilingual)

### 1. 悲伤 / 孤独 (Sad/Lonely)

**English:**
Slow piano solo, single piano, sparse single-note melody with pauses, large reverb like an empty room, occasionally isolated high notes falling. Mood: quiet, lost, melancholic.

**中文:**
慢速钢琴独奏，只用一架钢琴，稀疏的单音旋律和留白，混响开大，像空旷房间里录音。偶尔高音区落下孤立的单音。情绪：安静、失落。

---

### 2. 燃 / 热血 (Epic/Thrilling)

**English:**
Epic orchestral. First 15 seconds: timpani rolls and low strings铺垫; after 15 seconds: brass section unison melody, full percussion. Dense arrangement, no pauses. Mood: hero enters.

**中文:**
史诗管弦乐。前15秒定音鼓滚奏+低音弦乐铺垫；15秒后铜管组齐奏主旋律，打击乐全开。密集编排，不留白。情绪：英雄登场。

---

### 3. 温馨 / 治愈 (Warm/Healing)

**English:**
Acoustic guitar strumming solo, occasional high-register single-note melody. No electronic sounds, no percussion. Major key, relaxed tempo, neither rushed nor slow. Mood: relaxed, warm.

**中文:**
木吉他扫弦独奏，偶尔加高音区单音旋律。无电子音色、无打击乐。大调，节奏轻快不急促。情绪：放松、温暖。

---

### 4. 梦幻 / 空灵 (Dreamy/Ethereal)

**English:**
Synth pad foundation, music box high melody. Heavy reverb and delay, rhythm vague or no clear beat. Vocals (if any) breathy, like floating from far away. Mood: like dreaming.

**中文:**
合成器Pad铺底，加八音盒的高音旋律。大量混响和延迟，节奏模糊甚至无明确节奏。人声（如有）用气声，像从远处飘来。情绪：像在做梦。

---

### 5. 紧张 / 悬疑 (Tense/Suspense)

**English:**
Contrabass pizzicato, sustained synth bass, occasionally dissonant high string tremolo. Irregular rhythm, lots of empty space creating unease. Mood: calm before storm.

**中文:**
低音提琴拨奏+合成器持续低音，偶尔加入不协和的高音弦乐颤音。节奏不规则，留出大量空白制造不安。情绪：暴风雨前的宁静。

---

### 6. 快乐 / 活泼 (Happy/Playful)

**English:**
Ukulele or glockenspiel lead melody, light clapping or cajon percussion. Major key, bouncy rhythm, short crisp notes. Mood: bouncing in sunshine.

**中文:**
尤克里里或钢片琴为主，加轻快的拍手或木箱鼓。大调，节奏跳跃，短促的音符为主。情绪：像在阳光下蹦蹦跳跳。

---

### 7. 史诗 / 宏大 (Cinematic Narrative)

**English:**
Full orchestral, strings bed, brass melody, timpani accents. Slow tempo, clear structure: intro-development-climax-outro. Mood: movie opening credits rising.

**中文:**
完整管弦乐编制，弦乐群铺底+铜管旋律+定音鼓点缀。慢速，有明确的起承转合（引子-展开-高潮-尾声）。情绪：像电影片头字幕升起。

---
